What is COVID-19?

COVID 19 is abbreviation of CORONA VIRUS DISEASE -2019 (1st outbreak in Yuhan province of China in 2019). The name COVID is given bu WHO after its massive outbreak all over the world.

Understanding Your Risks

What are the Symptoms and sequelae of COVID-19?

Persistent symptoms after COVID-19 are not restricted to those who were critically ill or hospitalized. Rather, they can also occur in patients who had mild disease and never needed to be hospitalized.
